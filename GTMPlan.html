2. Target Market & Competitive Edge

A successful GTM strategy starts with a deep understanding of the target customer and the competitive landscape[citation:1][citation:7].

Primary Follower (B2C)

The "Eager Learner": New to crypto, lacks time/confidence to trade alone, suffers from FOMO and past losses. Seeks safe, guided entry.

Primary Master (B2B2C)

The "Strategic Guru": Experienced trader with a proven record. Seeks monetization, audience growth, and professional tools.

Competitive Position

We avoid direct feature wars with giants like Binance. Instead, we own the niche of Educative & Social Copy Trading, focusing on trust and community[citation:5].

3. Core Go-to-Market Strategy

Our launch is a phased, evidence-based campaign centered on acquiring and activating the most critical asset first: high-quality Masters[citation:10].

The "Learn-to-Earn" Hub Difference:

  • Post-Trade Analysis: Every copied trade comes with the Master's notes on the "why" (strategy, market context).
  • Virtual Portfolio Sandbox: Followers test-copy strategies with fake money before committing real capital.
  • Master Tier System: A transparent ladder (Standard → Verified → Elite → Founder) with clear benefits and risk-based requirements.

Hybrid Pricing & Monetization:

  • For Masters: Performance fee (share of follower profits). Founder Masters get a 90/10 split for the first year.
  • For Followers: Freemium model. Basic copy trading is free. "Pro Learn" subscription ($5-7/month) unlocks deep analytics, real-time signals, and advanced educational content.

Phase 1: Master Foundation (T-8 to T-4 Weeks)

Goal: Recruit & onboard 10-20 "Founder Masters."

  • Action: Secret outreach via LinkedIn/Discord to top traders. Offer exceptional terms (90/10 split, seed capital).
  • KPI: Signed contracts and completed KYC/verification.

Phase 2: Content & Community Build (T-4 to T-1 Weeks)

Goal: Generate social proof and pre-launch buzz.

  • Action: "Founder Master Bootcamp" to train on platform and content creation. Teaser campaigns on crypto forums.
  • KPI: 2,000+ waitlist sign-ups for followers.

Phase 3: Official Launch & Blitz (D-Day to D+14)

Goal: Maximize awareness and initial user activation.

  • Action: Coordinated launch: Press Release, CEO/KOL Livestream, Founder Master AMAs, targeted social ads.
  • KPI: 500 Masters, 5,000 Followers registered.

4. Execution & Channel Strategy

How we will reach our target audiences effectively[citation:1][citation:5].

  • Dedicated Support: Live chat for high-intent users.

5. KPIs & Measurement

Tracking the right metrics is critical to evaluate success and optimize the strategy post-launch[citation:10].

CAC
Customer Acquisition Cost

Must be significantly lower than LTV. Target: Follower CAC < $50.

LTV
Lifetime Value

Driven by subscription fees and platform profit share. Target LTV:CAC ratio > 3:1.

AUM Growth
Assets Under Management

The primary health metric of the platform. Target: $500K in 6 months.

6. Risk Management & Compliance

Proactive mitigation is non-negotiable in fintech and crypto[citation:8].

  • Regulatory First: Entity registered in a crypto-friendly jurisdiction (e.g., Singapore). Clear Terms of Service positioning us as a technology platform, not a fund manager.
  • Built-in Risk Controls: Mandatory stop-loss settings for followers, "Risk Shield" tools for Masters, transparent performance metrics (Sharpe Ratio, Max Drawdown).
  • Security & Trust: 95%+ cold storage for custodial assets, published SAFU (Secure Asset Fund for Users) fund, regular third-party security audits.
  • FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt) Plan: Pre-prepared communication templates for potential crises (e.g., a Master's major loss, market crash).
