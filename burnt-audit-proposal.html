HYPOTHESIS 1
Tracking architecture is not ready for paid campaigns
"LinkedIn Ads launched today would run blind for 2–4 weeks at 40–60% higher cost-per-lead."
01
What
we see

matters

Without a server-side layer to recover those signals, GA4 is underreporting real visitors. Every budget decision today is built on that incomplete picture.
