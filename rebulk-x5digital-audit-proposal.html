The strengths above are why Rebulk is ready to scale. The four findings below are why scaling hasn't started yet.

Four unconfirmed hypotheses — formed from observable signals only. If any one is accurate, it's a meaningful revenue gap. We can't know without data access. Which is exactly the point.

01
Hypothesis 01 — No foundation to scale paid acquisition
"The moment LinkedIn Ads launch, the bidding algorithm will run blind — and every iPhone buyer will be invisible."
01
What
we see
PostHog is the only tracking tool active on rebulk.com, loaded as 5 inline script files. No GTM container, no LinkedIn Insight Tag, no Meta Pixel, and no server-side GTM — which is the architecture that keeps tracking alive when iOS and Safari block client-side pixels.
02
Why it
matters
PostHog is a product analytics tool — built for session recording and click tracking, not marketing attribution. When LinkedIn Ads launch, Campaign Manager needs exactly one signal to optimize: "this person requested a demo." Without LinkedIn Insight Tag plus server-side conversion tracking, the bidding algorithm runs blind — and every demo request from a Safari or iPhone user is invisible.
