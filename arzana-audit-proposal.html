01
Hypothesis 01 — Tracking Architecture is Client-Side Only
"Your ads optimize on data missing 30–50% of the real conversions."
01
What
we see
GA4 measurement ID G-MZS0CGL6HN is pasted inline via googletagmanager.com/gtag/js — directly in source, not wrapped in any container. No GTM-XXXXXXX ID anywhere in HTML. No sGTM subdomain (metrics.arzana.com / sst.arzana.com) visible via DNS. No client-side conversion event fires when the Tally form submits or the Calendly booking completes — we watched the network tab.
02
Why it
matters
When tracking is client-side only, scripts load from third-party domains (facebook.net, linkedin.com, google-analytics.com) that iOS 14+ Safari ITP and ad blockers detect and block at the DNS layer. For a manufacturing executive ICP on iPhone in corporate environments, LinkedIn Insight Tag would be blocked entirely — not partially. Estimated 30–50% of conversion events never reach the ad platforms. The optimization algorithm is learning on a dataset missing a third of the signal.
