matters
HYPOTHESIS 1 — iOS and Safari are silently blocking a large share of your conversion data.

Patent attorneys are your primary ICP. They use Mac + Safari at higher rates than the average B2B buyer, and many operate under IT-enforced privacy policies. Safari's Intelligent Tracking Prevention blocks client-side tracking pixels at the browser level — the conversion event demo_form_submitted never fires, and the signal never reaches Meta or LinkedIn.

LinkedIn Ads don't receive enough conversion signals to exit the learning phase. Dreamdata — which you've invested in — is building attribution models on an incomplete dataset from the start.

The consequence: LinkedIn CAC appears inflated, budget gets reallocated away from your best channel, and the ICP-qualified pipeline shrinks while the problem stays invisible.
